## Consensus mechanism

Models fail the **instruction vs. artifact** boundary and regress to high-probability marketing English. Negative phrase bans + leak scanners + outcome-specific rewrites outperform “make it sound human” prompts. Word lists age; **vagueness, density, and internal leakage** remain the durable tells.
